    WHO AM I ?

    WHO AM I ?

    A design enthusiast, entrepreneur and mentor who believes that design will bring the next revolution in the lives of people.

    I have worked with companies, designers to improve their design processes to ensure that design solves the right problem and is user-friendly.

    Currently, I am helping FortyTwo Labs with the consumer and merchant app products like management and admin control consoles.

    A successful digital product solves the right problem for the right users. These products have complexities and high Investment, so understanding the user’s problem is crucial. I help brands and companies by validating the product-market fit early in the process. So that they can build products that matter to people and get traction.


    To understand and learn to create an impact on the users is what I seek to do in my Master’s degree.
